“Chuck Roth is a composer satirist and multimedia artist based in Brooklyn. Receiving his BA in Computer Science and Linguistics from Columbia University, he interrogates how language both processes the past and constructs possibilities for futures. His explorations of “semiotic grief” cover a wide variety of mediums including improvised performances, music, digital installations, zines, t-shirts and memes . He has performed at the Metropolitan Museum of Art, Carnegie Hall, Lincoln Center, and SXSW with Kembra Pfahler Wynton Marsalis Herland Riley and Esperanza Spalding.”
